Complete One Piece devil fruit guide

Devil fruit introduction

What is a devil fruit?

Devil Fruits are the most incredible form of power in the world of One Piece.These fruits grant immense strength to those who eat them, and in exchange for their newfound powers, they sacrifice the ability to swim.

Logia types

Logia Devil Fruits allow a person to create, control, and transform their body into an element of nature, depending on which element the fruit gives. The user can transform partially or completely. One of the greatest strengths of Logia fruits are the defensive capabilities that they have to offer.

Zoan types

Zoan-type Devil Fruits grant the ability to transform into a certain animal species. Unlike Paramecia and Logia Devil Fruits, Zoans are said to have "a will of their own," even before consumption. There are 3 types of zoan fruits:Normal, Mythical and Ancient

Normal zoans

Normal zoans transform you into a simple animal. For example: a jaguar, a girrafe.

Ancient zoans

Ancient zoans transform you into an ancient animal. All known Ancient zoans transform you into different species of dinosaurs.

Mythical zoans

Mythical zoan allow the user to transform into creatures who are the subject of a mith or legend. Let's for example say Kaido's devil fruit. It allows him to transform into a mythical dragon.

Paramecia types

The defining aspect of Paramecia fruits, in basic terms, is that they grant any power other than the ability to transform into an animal like a Zoan or a natural element like a Logia. Thus, the Paramecia class encompasses a massive range of varying and peculiar abilities with their own strengths and weaknesses. There is only one exception, Katakuri's devil fruit, the mochi mochi fruit. It is classified as a special paramecia, because it has the properties of a logia type.
