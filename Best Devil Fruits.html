Complete One Piece devil fruit guide

Best One Piece Devil Fruits

Logia Types

How do logias rank among devil fruit types?

Pretimeskip logia fruits were the strongest, but now, with Haki they have a counter. Even after that, they are still the strongest devil fruit tipe.

Top 5 strongest logia type devil fruits

5. The Mera Mera no mi (The Flame Flame Fruit)

The Mera Mera no mi is a Logia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create, control and transform into fire at will. It was previously eaten by Portgas D. Ace, and currently by Sabo. This fruit has insane distructive capabilities in the right hands. There are still better fruits and this is why i'm placing it in Nr. 5.

4. The Pika Pika no mi (The light light fruit)

The Pika Pika no Mi is a Logia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create, control, and transform into light at will, turning the user into a Light Human. It enables the user to move at the speed of light and transport themself by means of reflection. It was eaten by Borsalino, also known as Admiral Kizaru. It only has one weakness. Light can only go straight and that is the problem.

3. The Hie Hie no mi (The ice ice fruit)

The Hie Hie no Mi is a Logia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create, control, and transform into ice at will, turning them into a Freezing Human. It was eaten by Kuzan, better known as former Admiral Aokiji. This fruit does not have any major weaknesess.

2. Magu Magu no mi (The magma magma fruit)

The Magu Magu no Mi is a Logia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create, control, and transform into magma at will, turning the user into a Magma Human. It was eaten by Fleet Admiral Sakazuki, formerly known as Admiral Akainu.It's the fruit with the biggest distructive capabilities. This fruit also does not have any major weaknesess.

1. Yami Yami no mi (The darkness darkness fruit)

The Yami Yami no Mi is a Logia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create, control, and transform into darkness at will, making the user a Darkness Human. It was eaten by Marshall D. Teach, also known as "Blackbeard", who stole it from Commander Thatch of the Whitebeard Pirates' 4th division after murdering him. This devil fruit can create huge darkness vortexes in which it can suck anything. The biggest strength of this fruit is that it can stop other devil fruits from working by sucking their power. It has one weakness. You feel 10 times more pain, but for a strong user, this shouldn't be the biggest problem.

Zoan Types

How do zoans rank among devil fruit types?

Zoan fruits are probably the most stable devil fruit type. They are very strong and also give stong new abilities and fast regeneration time. I think that pretimeskip they are the weakest type, but now, they are mostly equal with paramecias. The mithical zoans on the other hand are probably the strongest devil fruits.

Top 5 strongest zoan type devil fruits

5. Hito Hito no mi, Model: Daibutsu

The Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Daibutsu is a Mythical Zoan-type Devil Fruit that allows its user to transform into a Daibutsu (a giant gold Buddha) at will. It was eaten by Sengoku, the former fleet admiral of the Marines. It is a very strong fruit with great distuction power, but there are still better zoans.

4. Inu Inu no Mi, Model: Okuchi no Makami

The Inu Inu no Mi, Model: Okuchi no Makami is a Mythical Zoan-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to transform into a hybrid and full version of an ancient wolf deity at will. It was accidentally eaten by Yamato.

3. The Tori Tori no mi, Model: Pheonix

The Tori Tori no Mi, Model: Phoenix is a Mythical Zoan-type Devil Fruit which bestows the power to transform into a phoenix (and phoenix hybrid) at will. It was eaten by Marco. Most significantly, the user can create their "Blue Flames of Resurrection" , English version: Blue Flame of Revival), also called "Flames of Regeneration". These light-blue flames enshroud the user when they transform, though the latter is not necessary for the flames to be conjured. Generally, while the blue flames do not burn, spread, or exude heat like normal flames do, their "special" nature makes them more potent than normal fire, proven when Marco overpowered and caused pain to Prometheus, a "special" fire homie made from Big Mom's soul.

2. Uo Uo no mi, Model Seiryu

The Uo Uo no Mi, Model: Seiryu is a Mythical Zoan-type Devil Fruit that allows its user to transform into a hybrid and full Azure Dragon at will. Described as the "strongest class of Devil Fruit", it was once a World Noble treasure before being eaten by Kaidou, after Big Mom had stolen it during the God Valley Incident 38 years ago.

1. Hito Hito no mi, Model Nika

Hito Hito No Mi, Model: Nika is a mythical zoan devil fruit which is currently possessed by Monkey D Luffy. It enables the user to get the powers of Sun God Nika (A.K.A Warrior of Liberation) and it gives the user a rubber like body. It's powers is limited by the user's imagination. It can turn everything it touches to have/be very rubbery or elastic properties. It can also produce any elements like lightning, fire, and ice if the user wants to. It can also make the user to be bigger or smaller.

Paramecia types

How do paramecias rank among devil fruit types?

Paramecia type devil fruits are very strong so I think that they are the second strongest devil fruit type.

Top 5 strongest paramecia type devil fruits

5. Doku Doku no Mi, Venom Venon Fruit

The Doku Doku no Mi has toxic powers that have the potential to instantly end anyone’s life. The user of this paramecia-type devil fruit has the ability to produce and control any kind of poison found on the entire planet. It is another devil fruit with poisonous powers that shouldn’t be meddled with. If the opponent doesn’t have an antidote, it’s game over for them. This fruit was eaten by the Impel Down warden, Magellan.

4. Mochi-Mochi no Mi, Mochi Mochi Fruit

The Mochi Mochi no Mi devil fruit enables the user to entirely transform into Mochi (Japanese rice cake) and manufacture it at will. This devil fruit power may not seem to be as potent on paper, but Katakuri made the most out of it and expertly displayed the capabilities you get with this special devil fruit. It is a paramecia devil fruit but the powers are definitely logia-like; which is why it is considered to be the only special paramecia devil fruit in One Piece.

3. Zushi Zushi no Mi, Press Press Fruit

One of the deadliest but underappreciated devil fruits is Fujitora’s Zushi Zushi no Mi. This devil fruit enables the user to create and control gravitational forces. For instance, the user can perform telekinetic-like actions such as pushing things or people front and back or levitating them up in midair at will. It is one of the most terrifying devil fruits as it has the capacity to destroy the world.

2. Gura Gura no Mi, Tremor Tremor Fruit

Inarguably due to its utterly devastating abilities, the Gura Gura no Mi is regarded as the strongest Paramecia devil fruit of all time. As Marine Admiral Sengoku stated, this demon fruit indeed has the ability to end the planet, and its abilities are well-known worldwide. It is terrifying how capable this devil fruit can produce earthquakes or shockwaves that can travel through any medium. Furthermore, in hand-to-hand combat, this devil fruit user can employ his seismic tactics to cause great damage. This fruit was in the hands of Whitebeard before Blackbeard managed to misteriously get it's powers.

1. Ope Ope no Mi, Op Op Fruit

Coming in first is Trafalgar Law’s Ope Ope no Mi, the most versatile devil fruit in the One Piece world. This paramecia-devil fruit has a unique ability not found in any other devil fruit, that of granting another person everlasting youth in return for the devil fruit user’s own life. This fruit’s powers include the ability to construct a dome area called “Room” and to freely control everything or anybody inside of it like a surgeon. Furthermore, this user can pull off miracles and cure incurable illnesses with the powers of this devil fruit. It is currently used by Trafalgar D. Water Law.
